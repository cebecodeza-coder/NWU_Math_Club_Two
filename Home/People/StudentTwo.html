BSc Computer Science and Statistics

Thabo Cele

by Webber Productions

Thabo Cele moves with intent. There’s little hesitation in how he approaches problems—only a steady focus on solving them. A student of computer science and statistics, his foundation was laid at Glenwood High School, where he distinguished himself early, earning 97% in NSC Mathematics. But achievement, for Cele, was never the endpoint; it was an invitation to go further.

He taught himself Python, not out of necessity, but curiosity—drawn to the logic of code and the freedom it offered to build, test, and refine ideas. Programming became more than a skill; it became a language through which he could think.

Now, that same drive is pointed toward a larger ambition: to work at the frontier of machine learning and artificial intelligence, and ultimately to build a company of his own. For Cele, the trajectory is clear—learn deeply, build boldly, and shape what comes next.
