BSc Actuarial Science

Benjamin van Zyl

by Webber Productions

Benjamin van Zyl thinks long-term. Even early on, at Helpmekaar Kollege, his ambitions were already taking shape—not just to enter the world of actuarial science, but to rise within it.

Now pursuing actuarial science, he approaches his studies with a clear sense of direction: toward the structured, high-stakes environments of insurance firms and banks, and ultimately toward leadership at the highest level.

For van Zyl, mathematics is more than calculation; it is discipline. A way of sharpening judgment, of learning how to weigh risk, anticipate outcomes, and solve problems where the answers are never obvious. That mindset extends beyond finance.

An interest in astronomy hints at a broader curiosity—a willingness to look outward, to understand systems both vast and precise. In both, the appeal is the same: patterns, prediction, and the challenge of making sense of complexity.
